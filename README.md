# 🏨 Hospitality Revenue & Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Problem Statement

In the hospitality industry, tracking revenue, occupancy, and booking trends is critical for maximizing profitability and optimizing resource utilization. This project analyzes hotel performance data to identify revenue drivers, occupancy patterns, and opportunities for pricing optimization.

---

## 📁 Dataset Overview

* Dataset includes:

  * Booking data
  * Revenue metrics
  * Hotel properties
  * Room occupancy
  * Time-based trends
* Data was cleaned and modeled for analysis using Power BI

---

## 📊 Key KPIs

* Total Revenue
* Occupancy Rate (%)
* Average Daily Rate (ADR)
* Revenue per Available Room (RevPAR)
* Booking Trends Over Time
* Property-wise Performance

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Revenue is highly dependent on occupancy rates and seasonal demand patterns
* Certain properties consistently outperform others, indicating location or pricing advantages
* Peak booking periods contribute significantly to overall revenue
* Variations in ADR across properties highlight opportunities for pricing optimization

---

## 🧠 Business Impact

* Enables hotels to **optimize pricing strategies (ADR)**
* Helps improve **occupancy and capacity utilization**
* Supports **revenue maximization decisions**
* Assists in identifying **high-performing and underperforming properties**
